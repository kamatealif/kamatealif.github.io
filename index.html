01.About Me

I care about systems that feel understandable from both sides: strong enough in the backend to solve a real problem, and simple enough in the frontend that a recruiter, user, or teammate can see the value quickly.

My Journey

I started my education in the BCA program at Shivaji University, Kolhapur. During my second year, I faced challenging financial conditions that required me to take a drop. However, I didn't let that time go to waste.

During that gap, I immersed myself in exploring web technologies and completed a rigorous Data Science course, building several practical projects along the way. I realized that in today's world, data is everything. This insight inspired me to pivot into the Data Science & AI field.

Leveraging my foundation in machine learning, I am now self-taught and driven, focusing on building robust AI architectures and improving my skills every day.
